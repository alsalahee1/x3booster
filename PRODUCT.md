# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Car owners, auto workshop operators, and fleet managers in Malaysia and the Middle East. They care about tangible engine performance and fuel economy — they are skeptical buyers who want proof, not polish. They discover X3 through WhatsApp recommendations, social sharing, and gas-station word-of-mouth. Mobile-first audience; many visit via a shared link.

## Product Purpose

X3 S-Performance is a two-product line of automotive chemical additives: a Fuel Booster that saves up to 35% on fuel and an Engine Oil Booster that reduces friction by 40%. The brand exists to prove that a single small bottle can deliver measurable, immediate results — backed by science, guaranteed by money-back. The website's job is to convert a skeptical viewer into a WhatsApp inquiry or purchase within one scroll session.

## Brand Personality

Aggressive. Precise. Proven. The brand is not lifestyle aspiration — it is performance credibility. Think Liqui-Moly meets Malaysian motorsport workshop: technically authoritative, visually dramatic, no fluff. The real logo (X3 / S-Performance) is bold and compressed; the design should honor that weight.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS-cream landing pages (Inter font, beige bg, rounded cards)
- Cheap auto-parts store aesthetics (cluttered, low-contrast, stock-photo overload)
- Oil-company corporate green (BP / Shell palette feel)
- Western lifestyle automotive (Castrol emotional advertising — this brand is technical and direct)

## Design Principles

1. **Proof over promise.** Show the dirty valve next to the clean one. Show the bottle going into the tank. Show the science. Claims without evidence are invisible to this audience.
2. **Drama earns trust.** The brand's own photography is cinematic — dark, high-contrast, amber/red accents. The design should match that energy, not soften it.
3. **One decisive move per section.** Not a gallery of proof — one dominant image per scroll beat, with text that earns its place next to it.
4. **The real logo is the logo.** Stop using styled text; the X3 S-Performance mark is a real brand asset and should be on every page header.
5. **Mobile conversion first.** The WhatsApp button and Add-to-Cart flow must be frictionless on a small phone. Desktop is secondary.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Body text must hit ≥4.5:1 contrast on dark backgrounds. The audience includes users who primarily read Arabic — the body font must support Arabic script (for any future bilingual copy). Respect prefers-reduced-motion.
